Standard on Every Vehicle
Every ride, the same high standard.
Fleet-Wide Secured Wi-Fi
High-speed secured Wi-Fi on every vehicle so riders can work, join calls, or stream without interruption from the moment they board.
USB and Power Charging
USB charging ports at every row plus power outlets on larger vehicles. Riders never arrive with a dead phone or laptop.
Real-Time GPS
Every vehicle tracked in real-time through our operations system and visible to riders via the CLS mobile app for iOS and Android.
Daily Safety Inspections
Every vehicle is inspected before each shift by an experienced mechanic checking brakes, tires, lights, fluid levels, and all safety systems.
Detailed Daily
On-site mechanics and detailers ensure every vehicle is spotless inside and out before it ever picks up a passenger.
EV and Hybrid Options
Electric and hybrid vehicles available for programs with sustainability mandates. Full GHG emissions reporting provided for any vehicle or route.
