Why CLS
A company that values your contribution.

Are you ready for a rewarding driving career in the Bay Area? We are looking for enthusiastic Class B Chauffeurs to provide an enjoyable, professional driving experience for some of the Bay Area top organizations.

CLS Global is a great place to work and grow. You will be part of a friendly, high-performing team where you will be respected as an individual and where excellent work is recognized and rewarded.

Benefits
Competitive pay with direct deposit
Competitive hourly rates commensurate with experience and class license. Reliable bi-weekly direct deposit with clear pay structure.
Paid vacation and time off
Accrued PTO from day one. We believe in work-life balance and making sure our team has time to recharge.
Health benefits package
Medical benefits available for full-time employees. We invest in the wellbeing of our team members.
Ongoing professional training
TSI-certified training, quarterly refresher sessions, and skill development opportunities. We invest in making you better at your craft.
Growth opportunities
Many of our operations managers and dispatchers started as drivers. CLS promotes from within and supports career advancement at every level.
