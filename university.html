Solutions
Built around your
campus, not ours.

CLS Global has experienced staff on-call 24/7/365 to help design an inter-campus program around your specific layout, schedule, and ridership needs.

Fixed Route Programs
Scheduled, reliable shuttle loops connecting key campus buildings, parking lots, housing, and transit connections on a consistent timetable.
On-Demand Service
Riders request a pickup instantly via mobile app. Perfect for smaller campuses or off-peak hours when fixed routes aren't efficient.
Inter-Campus Connectors
Connect multiple campus locations, satellite facilities, or research sites keeping faculty and staff moving between buildings without losing time.
Last Mile to Transit
Bridge the gap between BART, Caltrain, and bus stops to your campus entrance making public transit a realistic option for more of your community.
ADA & Accessibility
All programs can include ADA-compliant vehicles with ramp access, priority seating, and trained operators for passengers with mobility needs.
