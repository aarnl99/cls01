Employer Benefits
Why offer a shuttle program?
Attract and retain top talent
A shuttle program is a high-visibility perk that resonates with employees and candidates in competitive Bay Area hiring.
Employees arrive ready to work
No traffic stress, no parking frustration. Employees who commute by shuttle arrive calmer and more productive.
Reduce your carbon footprint
CLS currently takes over 4,000 cars off Bay Area roads every month. Each program includes full GHG emissions reporting.
Reduce parking pressure
Fewer employees driving means fewer parking spaces needed reducing infrastructure costs and campus congestion.

Professional, friendly drivers

Skip traffic entirely
