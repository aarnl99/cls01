Employer Benefits
Why offer a shuttle program?

A well-run shuttle system becomes a competitive advantage for recruiting, retention, productivity, and your carbon footprint.

Attract and retain top talent
A shuttle program is a high-visibility perk that resonates with employees and candidates especially in competitive Bay Area hiring.
Employees arrive ready to work
No traffic stress, no parking frustration. Employees who commute by shuttle arrive calmer, more focused, and more productive.
Reduce your carbon footprint
CLS currently takes over 4,000 cars off Bay Area roads every month. Each shuttle program comes with full GHG emissions reporting.
Reduce parking pressure
Fewer employees driving means fewer parking spaces needed reducing infrastructure costs and campus congestion significantly.
Employee Benefits
What riders love about it.

Employees who ride CLS shuttles consistently report higher satisfaction with their commute and their employer.

Work, rest, or recharge on the way
Secured Wi-Fi, USB charging at every seat, and dedicated workspace mean the commute becomes productive time or true downtime.
Real-time visibility via mobile app
Live GPS tracking, stop-by-stop ETAs, intelligent delay alerts, and e-ticketing all from iOS or Android.
Professional, friendly drivers
Our drivers are known for remembering riders by name and stop. Background-checked, TSI-certified, and trained for exceptional service.
Skip traffic entirely
Dedicated routes bypass the parking search and rush-hour crawl. Predictable departure times mean a predictable, stress-free commute.
