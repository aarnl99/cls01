Safety Programs
A culture of safety,
not just a policy.

At CLS Global, safety is not a department it's a culture. Every person in the organization understands its importance. Our programs are ongoing, not one-time.

Bus Rodeos Competitive Safety Training
All chauffeurs compete on difficult obstacle courses for the prize of best driver of the year. Real-world skill reinforcement in a high-stakes, competitive format.
Internal Safety Committee & Review Board
Weekly meetings to review incidents, identify risks, and ensure compliance across all operations. Every safety rule is enforced without exception.
Hours of Service Monitoring
Our HOS policy is more stringent than DOT guidelines. Every driver's hours are electronically monitored ensuring fully rested operators before every shift.
Road Observation & Ride Checks
Our safety department conducts regularly scheduled road observations and unannounced ride checks to monitor chauffeur performance in real conditions.
ADA Vehicle Operation
All chauffeurs are trained on ADA vehicle operation and maintenance including ramp systems, priority seating protocols, and sensitivity training.
11M+
Miles operated without a single passenger injury or major vehicle damage.
This is the result of a comprehensive safety culture not luck. Every mile reflects our investment in training, technology, and accountability.
Compliance
CHP Inspections & DMV Audits
We consistently pass all California Highway Patrol inspections and DMV audits. Full compliance documentation available on request for any client or government contract.
Periodic Retraining
Minimum 6 hours per quarter of refresher training for every chauffeur reinforcing defensive driving, client relations, and safety protocols.
Driver Training
The most thorough training
program in the industry.

After extensive interviews and background checks, applicants attend CLS Global's training program classroom and on-road sessions taught by two highly qualified instructors.

TSI-Certified Defensive Driving
Transportation Safety Institute certified instructors teach the most effective defensive driving techniques available the same standard used by major transit agencies.
Sensitivity & Accessibility Training
Empathy training for mobility-challenged clients, ADA vehicle operation certification, and disability awareness so every rider feels welcomed and accommodated.
Airport & Special Procedures
Dedicated training for airport pickups and drop-offs, client relations for VIP and event services, and vehicle safety inspection protocols.
Quarterly Refresher Training
A minimum of 6 hours per quarter of ongoing training reinforcing concepts, updating drivers on policy changes, and maintaining the highest performance standards.
CPR & First Aid Certified
All Class B chauffeurs are CPR and first aid certified so your riders have a trained first responder on board for every trip.
Background Screening
Comprehensive background checks before any applicant enters training. Driving record review, criminal screening, and reference verification zero compromises.
Technology
We stay miles ahead
of the pack.

From our GPS-powered mobile app to onboard camera systems technology drives safety, transparency, and rider satisfaction across every program.

Real-Time GPS Tracking
Live vehicle location accessible by every rider via the mobile app. Stop-by-stop route history and precise ETA visibility no more guessing at the curb.
Mobile App (iOS & Android)
Schedules and stops, real-time tracking, intelligent delay notifications, eFare ticketing, rider badging, capacity reporting, and street view of pickup locations.
Onboard Camera Systems
Cameras on every vehicle provide real-time data used for training, coaching, and incident investigation improving safety and holding everyone accountable.
Electronic HOS Monitoring
Driver hours are tracked electronically to standards more rigorous than DOT requirements. Zero exceptions automated alerts flag any compliance risk before it becomes a problem.
Fleet-Wide Secured Wi-Fi
Every vehicle in our fleet is equipped with secured Wi-Fi so riders can work, stay connected, and arrive at the office already productive.
Computerized Dispatch System
Fully computerized reservation and dispatch accurate job allocation, real-time driver assignment, and continuous rider notifications from pickup to drop-off.
Rider Feedback Systems
Onboard automated surveys and an in-app feedback channel ensure continuous improvement every complaint is tracked, triaged, and resolved with accountability.
Performance Reporting Dashboard
Clients receive daily, weekly, and monthly reports on on-time performance, ridership, route efficiency, and driver metrics all the data you need to run your program.
